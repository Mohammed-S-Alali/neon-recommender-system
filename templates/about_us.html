About us

In this capstone project, we developed a recommender system for IPTV in Jawwy service that provided by STC.

The performance of the system measured by RMSE and Hit-Rate since it is a recommender system that concerns the most relevant and watched items to the customer.

Python and JupyterLab are tools with Sklearn libraries for implementing the proposed recommender system. Also, build a content-based, collaborative filtering and popularity-Based recommender systems after doing EDA on the dataset.

As a result, the system aids to enhance the use experience in IPTV and keeps users interactive. By the end, the recommender system developed to use on Jawwy IPTV.
